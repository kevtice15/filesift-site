Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

FileSift is a Mac app that sorts files on your computer. We designed it to keep your data private.

What we collect

Nothing. FileSift does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. It has no account system and no analytics or tracking.

Your files

FileSift reads and moves the files and folders you choose, entirely on your Mac. Your files and their contents are never uploaded or sent anywhere. Folder access is stored locally (as macOS security-scoped bookmarks) only so the app can reopen the folders you picked.

Network

The app does not connect to the internet.

Third parties

None. There are no third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics services.

Changes

If a future version adds an optional online feature (for example, AI-assisted sorting), this policy will be updated to clearly describe what data that feature sends and how it is used, and such features will be opt-in.
