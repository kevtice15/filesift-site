Frequently asked

Does FileSift copy or move my files?

It moves them into the destinations you choose — and only after you press Sort Now and confirm the summary. If a name already exists at the destination, FileSift gives the file a unique name instead of overwriting.

What file types does it handle?

Images and videos by default. Flip on “Show all file types” to also sort PDFs, text, archives, and anything else — each shown with its system icon.

Does it send my photos anywhere?

No. Everything happens on your Mac. There’s no network connection, no account, and no analytics.

Can I undo a mistake?

Yes. Staged assignments are fully undoable before you commit, and your in-progress plan is saved if you quit and come back.

What macOS do I need?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later.
